Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Mind Beads ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and disclosed when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location data.

The App operates primarily on the user's device and does not transmit personal data to external servers.

However, certain non-personal information may be automatically collected by system services provided by the operating system, such as basic diagnostic information used to maintain app stability and performance.

2. In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional in-app purchases to unlock additional features.

All payments and transactions are securely processed through the Apple App Store using Apple’s payment infrastructure.

The App does not collect, process, or store payment information, including credit card numbers or billing details.

For more information about how purchase data is handled, please refer to Apple's privacy policy.

3. Local Data Storage

The App may store certain information locally on your device in order to provide functionality and improve user experience. This may include:

  • app settings
  • bead selections
  • usage counters
  • user preferences

This information remains stored on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not currently use third-party services for analytics, advertising, or data collection.

If such services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. Since most data is stored locally on the user's device and not transmitted to external servers, the risk of external data exposure is minimized.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date.
